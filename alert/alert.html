All document supply services: scan on demand, order documents outside Geneva (SLSP Courier and ILL) as well as order of documents from the UNIGE library for other libraries are suspended from 24 December 2020 to 3 January 2021.

From 3 January 2021, from Monday to Friday between 19:00 and 22:00 and on Sundays, the Library will be accessible only to the UNIGE/HUG community. The general public will not be able to borrow or return documents during these periods.
